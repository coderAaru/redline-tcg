Redline TCG — System Specification & Blueprint
Master Production Blueprint — Version 10.0

📋 System Overview
Game Name: Redline TCG
Platform: Web Application (Responsive, Mobile-friendly Web UI)
Theme: Dark-mode, high-octane racing aesthetic (Sleek dark grays, carbon textures, neon red accent colors)
Core Loop: Earn Coins → Buy/Win Modifiers → Manage Inventory → Open Packs (Garage Tax) → Collect Cars → Discover New Cards (Discovery Rewards) → Scrap Duplicates / Complete Sets → Earn More Coins

🖥️ 1. Architecture & Page Layouts
The application features a persistent, responsive utility navigation architecture connecting seven distinct core interface systems:

Page 1: Pack Selection (The Showroom)
- Display Logic: Layout architecture grids all available card pack modules, sorted in ascending unlock cost order.
- Locked Packs: Rendered in a desaturated/monochrome filter overlay. Shows unlock cost alongside a compact, high-contrast dynamic opening fee label inside the footer. Clicking triggers the unlock confirmation gate.
- Unlocked Packs: Rendered in full color. The footer actions contain a compact green "UNLOCKED" status badge and a compact red "Opening Fee: X Coins" label aligned side-by-side above the "Rip Pack" button. Font sizes and padding are optimized to prevent layout crowding.

Page 2: Pack Opening (The Garage Bay)
- Staging Canvas: A clean cinematic staging canvas optimized for unpacking randomized assets.
- Rip Pack Mechanism: Triggers sequential package-tearing animations. Deducts a dynamic sequential opening fee per roll (from 1 Coin up to 17 Coins) depending on the pack's index, waived if a Free Pack Rip Ticket is active or a Tax-Exemption Permit is equipped.

Page 3: The Collection Log (The Index)
- Catalog Grid: A clean matrix index tracking every vehicle, filtered by pack, ordered from least to most rare.
- Card Duplicity Tracker: Vehicle asset slots display a persistent integer overlay (e.g., x3) tracking exactly how many duplicates of that specific vehicle card the player currently holds.
- Discovered Cards: Rendered with full chromatic fidelity, displaying photographic profile assets, official technical specs, set index, and set name.
- Undiscovered Cards: Hidden behind a solid black matte structural vehicle silhouette mask.
- Scrap/Sell Economy: Players can liquidate any card from their active inventory. Liquidating the final copy of a vehicle sets its discovery state to false, completely clearing it from their active garage collection.
- Bulk Scrap Duplicates: A prominent global batch-processing button labeled "Scrap All Duplicates" normalizes duplicate counts to x1. Adjacent to it, a dynamic yield tracker displays the total cumulative Coins the player stands to earn in parentheses—for example: Scrap All Duplicates (+245 Coins).

Page 4: The Performance Shop
- Currency Counter: Persistent top-of-screen balance tracker.
- Purchasable SKUs: Permanent unlocks of advanced card pack licenses or single-use Luck Charms.
- Discount Integration: If a 10% Off Shop Ticket is active, prices are displayed slashed with a strike-through and a yellow discounted cost.

Page 5: My Garage Inventory
- Storage Canvas: A dedicated screen holding inactive charms and utility tickets purchased from the shop or won from the spin wheel.
- Manual Deployments: Items sit inactive until manually toggled or equipped. Activating a charm equips it and starts its decay timer/durability countdown. Active tickets (10% Off, Free Pack Rip) apply to the next eligible transaction.

Page 6: Daily Spin Wheel
- Cooldown Limit: One spin per 24 hours (unless "Spin Again" is rolled).
- Physics Simulation: CSS-driven smooth rotation, pointing to one of 12 wedges.
- Outcomes: +10/25/50 Coins, -10/20 Coins, +10 & Spin Again, Free Pack Rip Ticket, Extra Card Charm, Charm Shop Lock (charmsDisabledToday), 10% Off Ticket, Universal Magnet Charm, Flat Tire.

Page 7: Newsroom & Live Events
- News Feed: Chronological feed displaying game updates, balance shifts, patches, and upcoming drop announcements.
- Event Spotlights: Live banners tracking calendar-based modifiers (e.g., active countdown for Easter Luck Boost or Halloween Week).

📦 2. Card Pack Structure & Overlap Logic
Vehicles are strategically mapped to thematic groupings. A single card asset can map into multiple packs concurrently if metadata criteria overlap, maximizing collection options.

🏎️ 3. Card Data Model & Statistics
The underlying database conforms to a strict structural schema. Pack eligibility lists 17 distinct packs sorted from lowest potential earner to highest potential earner. Mass-market packs target a minimum depth of 40 unique vehicles, and luxury/specialty packs target a minimum depth of 30 unique vehicles.

🎨 4. Pokémon-Inspired Card Layout & Visual Design UI
To deliver an authentic trading card feeling, the visual layout conforms to:
- Top Left Field: Manufacturer & Model text.
- Top Right Field: Uncapped dynamic performance rating gauge (OVR).
- Center Frame: Graphic asset with border shading and glowing aura matching the rarity tier, plus a duplicate counter overlay.
- Center Lower Body: A 2x2 technical spec dashboard.
- Footer: ID identifier left, and catalog set index right.

🧮 5. Overall Score (OVR) Calculation Engine
Calculated using the formula:
OVR = horsepower + top_speed + OVR(a) + OVR(q) + OVR(r)
- OVR(a) = (6 / a) * 100
- OVR(q) = (14 / q) * 100
- OVR(r) [Rarity Modifier]: Common (-100), Uncommon (-75), Special (-50), Rare (-25), Very Rare (0), Epic (+25), Legendary (+50), Exotic (+75), Limited Edition (+100).

💎 6. Rarity & Pull Probability Distributions
- Common (49.40%), Uncommon (26.50%), Special (12.50%), Rare (6.00%), Very Rare (4.00%), Epic (1.20%), Legendary (0.30%), Exotic (0.10%).
- Magnet charm skews pull rate towards undiscovered cards below Epic rarity with a 35% force roll chance.
- Honda Pack Adjustments: Epic drops suppressed to 0.50% baseline (and consecutive Epics suppressed to 0% in a single opening sequence), shifting weight to Uncommon and Special.
- BMW and German Pack Value Compression: Rare/Very Rare/Epic drop odds are significantly compressed and shifted to Uncommon and Special.
- Supercars Pack "Floor" Restructuring: Features a minimum drop floor of Rare or Very Rare cards. Worst-case scenario pull array yields a liquidation value below the 17 Coins opening fee, preventing infinite rolling loops.

⚙️ 7. Core Game Mechanisms & Economy

Dynamic Discovery Rewards
Discovering a vehicle for the first time awards Coins mapped to its rarity:
- Common, Uncommon, Special, Rare, Very Rare: +3 Coins
- Epic: +10 Coins
- Legendary: +20 Coins
- Exotic: +50 Coins
Duplicates yield 0 Coins on discovery. Free Starter Pack discoveries are hard-capped to a maximum ceiling of 50 Coins total.

Set Completion Bonuses
Achieving 100% completion of any pack's collection index awards a one-time bonus of +7 Coins. Re-completing after liquidating does not trigger duplicate bonus awards.

Garage Tax (Pack Opening Fee)
The opening fee scales dynamically by exactly +1 Coin for each sequential pack along the 17-pack progression line (Pack 1: 1 Coin; Pack 2: 2 Coins; ... Pack 17: 17 Coins). Waived if a Free Pack Rip ticket is active or a Tax-Exemption Permit is equipped.

Scrap/Liquidation Economy
Duplicate cards can be scrapped directly from the Index page:
- Sell Common: +1 Coin, Uncommon: +2 Coins, Special: +3 Coins, Rare: +10 Coins, Very Rare: +15 Coins, Epic: +25 Coins, Legendary: +50 Coins, Exotic: +100 Coins.

Total Liquidation Logic
Safeguard rules are removed. The player can scrap any card they own. Liquidating the last copy decrements its count to 0 and marks discovered = false.

Bulk Scrap Duplicates Operation
A "Scrap All Duplicates" button normalizes all duplicates to x1.

🎒 8. Garage Inventory & Duration Recalibration
- Epic Catalyst Charm: Grants +1.0% Epic drop boost for exactly 5 active minutes (300s).
- Legendary Catalyst Charm: Grants +0.5% Legendary drop boost for exactly 5 active minutes (300s).
- Exotic Catalyst Charm: Grants +0.2% Exotic drop boost for exactly 5 active minutes (300s).
- Universal Magnet Charm: Extended to exactly 10 active minutes (600s).
- Manufacturer Brand Charms: Ford and Nissan magnets are completely removed.
- High-Roller Charm: Suppresses Common drops entirely for next 2 rolls, shifting weight to Uncommon/Special/Rare.
- Tax-Exemption Permits: Waives opening fee for next 5 rolls.
